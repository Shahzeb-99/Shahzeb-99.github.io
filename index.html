Know Your Flag

Test and improve your knowledge of flags from around the world.

Overview

Know Your Flag is an educational and fun mobile game that helps you learn about flags from different countries. The game features two modes: a Free Mode where you can learn about flags at your own pace and a Scored Mode where you can test your knowledge against the clock. The game also includes a leaderboard where you can compare your score with players from around the world.
